## 4. On-Page SEO Checklist for Each Article

- [ ] Target keyword in H1 (exact or close match)
- [ ] Title tag: 50-60 chars with keyword
- [ ] Meta description: 150-160 chars with CTA
- [ ] Internal links: 3-5 to relevant casino pages
- [ ] FAQ section with schema markup
- [ ] "Related Casinos" section (affiliate links)
- [ ] Author bio with expertise
- [ ] Image alt text with keywords
- [ ] "Last Updated" date (updated regularly)

## 7. Seasonality & Timing

### Peak Seasons
- **December-January:** New Year resolutions, bonus seeking (HIGH)
- **March-April:** Purim related searches (MEDIUM)
- **Summer (June-August):** Mobile gaming increases (MEDIUM)
- **High Holiday Season:** Content refresh needed (MEDIUM)

### Content Update Schedule
- Evergreen content: Review & refresh quarterly
- Seasonal content: Update 2 weeks before peak season
- Casino reviews: Update immediately when bonus changes
- "Best of" posts: Refresh annually before peak season
